Frequently Asked Questions

How do I enroll in a course?

Simply click on any course you're interested in and click the "Enroll Now" button. You'll be prompted to create an account or login if you already have one.

Are there any free courses available?

Yes! We offer several free courses across different categories. Look for the "Free" badge on course cards, or filter by price in the courses section.

Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?

We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for all paid courses. If you're not satisfied with your purchase, contact our support team within 30 days for a full refund.

How long do I have access to a course?

Once you enroll in a course, you have lifetime access to the course materials, including any future updates.
